About me

Hello! My name is Iryna

My journey in video editing began with working at three professional companies specializing in creating wedding films. This gave me not only technical skills, but also a deep understanding of how to convey emotions, the atmosphere of the day, and the couple's true feelings through editing.

I sincerely love my work and treat each project with the utmost attention. It is important to me that each video is not just edited, but tells a story that you want to return to again and again.

I work with weddings from different cultures and traditions: American, African American, Indian, Norwegian, Swedish, Scottish, and others. This allows me to easily adapt my editing style to any audience and client's wishes.

I edit:

  • teasers
  • highlights
  • full films

I value deadlines, am always available, and communicate openly with clients. My goal is for you to receive a video that exceeds your expectations and becomes a priceless memory for a lifetime.
