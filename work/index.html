Work process

01.

Brief + File Delivery

You fill out the brief and include a Google Drive or Dropbox link with all your footage. In the brief, you specify the editing style, preferences, references, and all important details — this helps me clearly understand your vision and start working without unnecessary back-and-forth.

02.

Review & Confirmation

I review the brief and footage, confirm the scope of work, timeline, and readiness to begin the edit.

03.

Deposit Payment

After all details are approved, you submit a deposit via PayPal or Payoneer, and I start the editing process.

04.

Review & Revisions

You receive a Frame.io link to review the video and leave time-coded comments. I carefully and promptly apply the requested revisions.

05.

Final Delivery

Once the final version is approved, you receive the finished videos and, if needed, the project files for download.
